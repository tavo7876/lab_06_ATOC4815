# lab_06_ATOC4815
  Running this file pulls the data from March 14, 2024. I decided to find a day that had lots of rain to see the dewpoint depression increase over time. Witch is what we see in the blue. This data was resampled with the mean of every hour to simplify the data then calculated with dewpoint and temperature. It gives us a good idea for when there could be higher moisture in the air representing rain,fog, or dew. After this I decided to graph the realtive humidity(in red) on top of dewpoint depression to see the relationship throughout the day. When dewpoint depression decreases then relative humidity increases in a inverse relationship.
